The build

The 9 sessions

Each session is anchored to one book and produces one concrete build output. No fluff, no busywork.

01
Strategy choice

Playing to Win

By A.G. Lafley and Roger Martin

Build output: Strategy choice cascade, where to play and how to win, plus explicit tradeoffs.

02
Problem clarity

Good Strategy Bad Strategy

By Richard Rumelt

Build output: The strategy kernel, diagnosis, guiding policy, and coherent actions.

03
Outcome focus

Escaping the Build Trap

By Melissa Perri

Build output: Product outcomes scorecard with a single primary outcome and supporting inputs.

04
Product ownership

Inspired

By Marty Cagan

Build output: Product operating model, roles, decision rights, and escalation path.

05
User purpose

Jobs to Be Done

By Anthony Ulwick

Build output: Two job maps, one for First 5 and one for Bible studies, plus a handoff definition.

06
Messaging and promise

Value Proposition Design

By Alex Osterwalder and team

Build output: Two value maps, promise, pains, gains, plus clear boundaries for each product.

07
Metrics discipline

Lean Analytics

By Alistair Croll and Benjamin Yoskovitz

Build output: North star metric definition for each product, measurement plan, and reporting cadence.

08
Delivery model

Team Topologies

By Matthew Skelton and Manuel Pais

Build output: Build plan, team structure, interaction model, and vendor boundaries.

09
Decision cadence

The Advantage

By Patrick Lencioni

Build output: Leadership decision cadence, review rhythm, and a one page decision log template.

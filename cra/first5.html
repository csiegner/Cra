From Product Paralysis to Product Clarity

A decision forcing workflow for leaders who need to define what their product is, what it is not, and how to move forward with confidence.

Who This Is For

This workflow is for a product or platform leader inside an established organization who owns a live digital product with real users, real costs, and real pressure to decide what comes next.

  • You own an app or platform that has lost clarity of purpose
  • Engagement is declining or growth has stalled
  • Vendor cost and technical debt are limiting progress
  • Conversations repeat without durable decisions

What Problem This Solves

When product purpose blurs, everything slows down. Teams debate features instead of outcomes. Costs rise. Rebuild conversations start before clarity exists.

This workflow replaces brainstorming with decision ready artifacts that leadership can approve, reject, or act on.

The 9 Session Curriculum

Session 1

Choose or Drift

Make explicit strategic choices about where the product plays and how it wins.

Session 2

Name the Real Problem

Strip away symptoms and define the core problem blocking progress.

Session 3

Outcomes Before Features

Shift from shipping features to owning outcomes.

Session 4

Who Owns the Product

Clarify decision rights, accountability, and ownership.

Session 5

What Job Is This Doing

Define what each product is hired to do by the user.

Session 6

Promise and Boundary

Define value clearly and establish non negotiable boundaries.

Session 7

Pick the One Metric

Select a single primary success metric for each product.

Session 8

Design for Delivery

Align execution model, team structure, and build versus buy decisions.

Session 9

Decide and Move

Create a leadership cadence that prevents drift from returning.
